Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 10, 2026

Tapey is built around one principle: your photos stay on your device. This policy explains what data is collected, by whom, and how it is used.

1. Introduction

Tapey ("we", "our", "the app") is an iPhone app that lets you arrange your photos into a gallery-wall wallpaper. Your privacy matters to us. We do not run servers, we do not store your photos, and we do not know who you are.

2. Data We Collect

2a. Data collected by us

None. Photos you add, wallpapers you create, and app settings are stored only on your device (in device storage / IndexedDB). They are never transmitted to any server, never seen by us, and are deleted when you uninstall the app.

We have no user accounts, no registration, and no analytics SDK of our own.

2b. Data collected by Google AdMob (third party)

Tapey is free and supported by ads. When you save a wallpaper, a short interstitial ad may appear, served by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect:

  • Device identifier (IDFA — Identifier for Advertisers)
  • App interaction data (e.g. ad impressions, taps)
  • Crash and diagnostic data

This data is collected and processed by Google, not by us. Google's privacy policy applies: policies.google.com/privacy.

IDFA is only used for personalised ads if you grant permission via the App Tracking Transparency (ATT) prompt (see Section 4). If you decline, only non-personalised ads are shown and IDFA is not used for targeting.

3. How Data Is Used

We do not use any data because we do not collect any.

Google AdMob uses the data it collects to serve and measure ads, improve ad quality, and for fraud prevention. We receive only aggregate, anonymised revenue reports — no personally identifiable information.

4. App Tracking Transparency (ATT)

On first launch, iOS will show a system prompt asking whether Tapey may track your activity across other apps and websites. This permission controls whether your IDFA can be used for personalised advertising by AdMob.

  • If you allow: AdMob may use your IDFA to show ads that are more relevant to you.
  • If you decline: Only non-personalised ads are shown. AdMob may still collect non-identifying data (e.g. crash reports).

You can change this choice at any time in Settings › Privacy & Security › Tracking.

5. Data Retention & Deletion

All data stored by Tapey (your photos, settings, and wallpaper history) exists only on your device and is deleted automatically when you uninstall the app.

Data collected by Google AdMob is retained per Google's own policies. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for details on retention and deletion.

6. Children's Privacy

Tapey is rated 4+ on the App Store. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Because we collect no data ourselves, there is no special risk to children from our side. AdMob's child-directed settings are respected per Google's policies.

7. Contact Us

Questions about this privacy policy? Please reach out — we read every message.

Email: kvndh66@gmail.com

Support: kvndh.github.io/support/tapey.html

8. Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we will update this page and revise the effective date at the top. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
