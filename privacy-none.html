English

No Data Collected

This app does not collect any personal information and does not transmit data to external servers.

Data Stored on Device

The following data is stored only on your device:

  • App settings and presets
  • Usage history (stored in local UserDefaults)
  • App preferences (theme, language, sound, etc.)

All data is deleted when you uninstall the app.

In-App Purchases

Payment processing is handled entirely by Apple.
The developer has no access to payment-related personal information.
