Tapey turns your photos into a washi-tape style gallery wall wallpaper — arranged automatically, edited intuitively, and saved straight to your iPhone's camera roll.

FAQ

How do I add photos?
Tap the + button on the empty wall screen, then choose photos from your library or take a new one with your camera. You can add up to 12 photos per wallpaper.
How do I save the wallpaper?
Tap Save (or the save icon in the toolbar). The wallpaper is exported at full resolution and saved directly to your iPhone's photo library. A short ad may appear before saving — see below for why.
Why does an ad appear when I save?
Tapey is completely free. To keep it free, a short full-screen ad appears once per save. The ad never blocks your save — if it fails to load, your wallpaper is saved immediately anyway.
Are my photos uploaded to a server?
No. All photo processing happens entirely on your device. Your photos are never uploaded, transmitted, or stored anywhere outside your iPhone.
How do I set the wallpaper on my iPhone?
After saving, open Settings → Wallpaper → Add New Wallpaper, pick Photos, and select the image Tapey saved. Choose the lock screen or home screen slot and tap Set.
The app isn't working or it crashed — what should I do?
Try force-quitting and relaunching the app. If the problem persists, make sure you are on the latest version from the App Store. If it still happens, please email us with a description of what you were doing — we respond quickly.
Can I use Tapey on iPad?
Tapey is designed for iPhone. It will run on iPad in iPhone compatibility mode, but the layout is optimised for phone screens.
